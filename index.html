Biography

I am a master's student at National Taiwan University, working with Professor Shao-Hua Sun at the National Taiwan University Robot Learning Lab (NTURLL). My research focuses on imitation learning from suboptimal demonstrations, language-guided robot learning, and generative models for embodied control. I am passionate about building robotic systems that can learn from language and structured knowledge, with the hope that these technologies may one day help people in meaningful ways.

I have been saved by my Lord Jesus Christ since 2018, and my faith shapes every part of my life. Through His sacrifice on the cross, He forgave my sins and gave me a new life filled with grace and purpose. Since then, I have sought to follow Him and live in a way that reflects His love and truth. The Lord has given me opportunities to serve in the youth ministry of Banner Church. I pray that God would use both my life and my research for His kingdom—to serve those in need, to bless others through the gifts He has given, and ultimately to spread the Gospel and bring glory to His name.
