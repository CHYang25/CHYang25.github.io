Biography

I have been a follower of Jesus Christ since 2018, and my faith profoundly shapes every aspect of my life. Jesus is my Lord and Savior, the Way, the Truth, and the Life. Through His sacrifice on the cross, He forgave my sins, cleansed my heart, and gave me a new life filled with grace and purpose. I believe that through Him alone we can come to the Father, and I seek to live a life that reflects His love and truth. The Lord uses me to serve Him in the youth ministry of Banner Church, where I help guide and support the younger generation in their walk with Christ.

Academically, I am a master's student at National Taiwan University, working with NTU Robot Learning Lab. I am passionate about research in language-guided robot learning and diffusion policies. I'm especially interested in using language feedback to improve policy learning and leveraging suboptimal offline data for diffusion-based methods.
